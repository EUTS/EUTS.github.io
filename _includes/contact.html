What's the difference?

Full Member

  1. Can organise events, workshops and meetings
  2. Can contact speakers
  3. Can be a leader for a group (ex. game development group or mobile development group)
  4. Can run for office positions
  5. Receives 8 value points for being a club member + value points for participating in the partial member activities (listed below)

Partial Member

  1. Can participate in challenges and projects
  2. Can write and post blog posts to the website
  3. Can contribute to the website through open source
  4. Can give suggestions for events or workshops
  5. Can attend workshops
  6. Receives points depending on the level of involvement
